Hi, I'm Swapnesh, I design games, playful interactions and do design research. I occasionally also write about things I read, observe and some unpopular opinions on games, play, design, art, and technology. Writing helps me put my thoughts together, reflect and clear the chaos (or does it?).

My current interest is in exploring games as a form of non-verbal communication, to express, explore and learn complex ideas in an abstract and creative ways. I support coexistence, absurdism and the grasshopper.

Recently, I've started to learn creative coding, I upload some of my hand drawn and generative sketches on a tumblr blog.
