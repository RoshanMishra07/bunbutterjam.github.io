Hi, I'm Swapnesh, I design games, interactions and do design research. I occasionally also write about things I read, observe and some unpopular opinions on games, play, design, art, and technology. Writing helps me put my thoughts together, reflect and clear the chaos (or does it?).

I mostly work independent on projects with studios, clients and in collaboration with other designers, artists, researchers, and developers. My current interest is in exploring games as a form of non-verbal communication, to express, explore and learn complex ideas in an abstract and creative ways.

Recently, I've started to learn p5, I upload some of my hand drawn and generative sketches on a tumblr blog. I'm also learning to get better at chess, you can send me a challenge on lichess.
