Discover Gandhi is an online platform launched by NCERT on the 150th Birth Anniversary of Mahatma Gandhi. It is a collection of interesting information, articles and multi-media resources based on the life of Mahatma Gandhi. It's made for school teachers and students to take part in different activities like competitions, quizzes and discussions on various topics related to Gandhi's life, and ideas.

I worked as a User Experience Designer on the project for MASSPL, who developed the website. My role was to design the information architecture, interactions and the interface.
